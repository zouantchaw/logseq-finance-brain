### Query Examples

**Get all expenses for current month:**

```clojure
[:find ?date ?amount ?merchant
 :where
 [?b :block/properties ?props]
 [(get ?props :type) ?type]
 [(= ?type "expense")]
 [(get ?props :date) ?date]
 [(>= ?date "2025-01-01")]
 [(<= ?date "2025-01-31")]
 [(get ?props :amount) ?amount]
 [(get ?props :merchant) ?merchant]]
```
